## Fonts

### Aquiline Two
- **Designer:** Manfred Klein
- **Used for:** Tom's handwriting (the quill's reply hand).
- **License:** Free font. Manfred Klein offers his typefaces free for personal
  and commercial use, with a request to donate to a children's charity. His
  licensing is stated informally on the distributing sites rather than as a
  formal text license.
- **Source:** https://www.dafont.com/aquiline-two.font
- **Note for forkers:** we redistribute `AquilineTwo.ttf` under those free-use
  terms. If you plan to redistribute commercially, confirm the current terms
  with the author/source, since they are informal.

## Inspiration

The quill/ink pipeline is a conceptual re-implementation of the reMarkable
`riddle` project by Maxime Rivest — https://github.com/MaximeRivest/riddle —
reimagined for iPad with an added memory subsystem.
